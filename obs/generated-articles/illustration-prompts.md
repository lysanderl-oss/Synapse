# Synapse 漫画插画 Prompt 清单

## 通用风格指令（每个prompt前面都加这段）

```
Style: Flat business illustration, modern cartoon style similar to Notion/Slack brand illustrations.
Color palette: primarily Gold #FCAD2A, Deep Blue #013A7D, Cyan #028CDC, Dark #0A1628, with white and light gray accents.
Characters: Cute chibi/Q-style (big heads, small bodies, 2-3 head-to-body ratio), expressive eyes, no nose detail, simple but clear facial expressions.
Line style: No outlines or very thin outlines, filled with flat color blocks.
Background: Clean, simple gradient or solid color, not cluttered.
Overall feel: Professional yet warm, like corporate brand illustrations from tech companies.
No text in the image. Wide aspect ratio (16:9).
```

## 场景1: 团队架构全景（Hero区主图）

```
A wide panoramic illustration showing an AI team hierarchy.

At the top center stands a confident leader figure in gold/amber clothing on a subtle elevated platform, representing the President/CEO. Below him, a blue-suited AI CEO figure acts as the bridge, with glowing connection lines spreading outward.

Around the CEO, arranged in a semi-circle, are multiple small team clusters:
- Left group (4 figures in gold tones): Think Tank / Advisory team, one holds a chart, another holds a telescope
- Center-left group (7 figures in cyan): Project delivery team with construction/IoT icons
- Center-right group (5 figures in blue): Tech/development team with laptop/code icons
- Right group (4 figures in dark blue): Operations team with gears/checklist icons
- Additional small groups scattered: content team, growth team, HR team

The background has subtle neural network / synapse connection lines in very low opacity, connecting all the teams. A few floating data nodes glow softly.

The overall composition should feel like a vibrant, organized ecosystem - not chaotic but interconnected. The atmosphere is collaborative and futuristic.
```

**用途**：首页 Hero 区背景图

## 使用说明

1. 在 claude.ai 网页版中使用以上prompt生成图片
2. 每个prompt前加上通用风格指令
3. 生成后下载PNG（建议1920x1080或更高分辨率）
4. 存放到网站 `/public/illustrations/` 目录
5. 文件命名：`scene-1-team.png`, `scene-2-review.png`, 以此类推
