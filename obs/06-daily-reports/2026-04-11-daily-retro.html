◆ Strategic Insight of the Day
gstack = Execution Engine. Synapse = Management OS.

They are complementary, not competing. gstack excels at individual developer velocity — Think/Plan/Build/Review/Test/Ship. Synapse excels at team orchestration — Decision/Dispatch/Knowledge/Governance.

Today's key upgrade: instead of using gstack as a standalone tool, we decomposed its methodologies and fused them into each RD team member's DNA. This is a higher-level integration — the team now carries gstack-grade execution capability within the Synapse framework, activated by 5 dedicated /dev-* commands.

One sentence: The president sets the goal, Lysander dispatches, the RD team executes with gstack-grade methodology, QA gates ensure quality, and knowledge feeds back into OBS. Full loop. Zero gaps.
